Article Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment Types

  • Compound: Normalized score between -1 and 1 that combines all aspects.
    • -1 = very negative
    • 0 = neutral
    • 1 = very positive
  • Positive: Proportion (0-1) of text expressing positive sentiment
  • Negative: Proportion (0-1) of text expressing negative sentiment
  • Neutral: Proportion (0-1) of text expressing neutral sentiment
